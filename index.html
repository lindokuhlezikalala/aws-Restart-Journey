ABOUT

Zoe is the founder of Zoe Fitness and is passionate about empowering others to take back their health!Zoe addresses health and fitness from a holistic approach by taking inventory of all the factors that affect optimal health. you will learn how to fuel your body, own your workouts,balance your hormones, and heal your metabolism.

EXPERIENCE AND QUALIFICATIONS

Zoe holds a Bachelor's degree in Exercise Science and is a certified personal trainer through the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM). With over 5 years of experience in the fitness industry, she has helped numerous clients achieve their health and fitness goals.
